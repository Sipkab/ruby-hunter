Ruby Hunter Privacy Policy

The game Ruby Hunter collects no personal information.

The game records your gameplay in order to provide the following community features:

  • Level completion leaderboards
  • Level statistics

These recordings are used to place your results in a shared leaderboard which is available for other players to view. You will appear on the leaderboard with the nickname of your choosing. (Or with an automatically generated nickname)

Other players are able to download and view the recording for your leaderboard entry therefore replay and view your gameplay.

Any levels you upload to the community server will be available for others to download.

Deletion of your user data

To request the deletion of your user data from our servers, please write an e-mail to the rubyhunter@sipka.dev address.
In this e-mail please include your intent to have your data deleted as well as the user name that you use with Ruby Hunter. The user name can be found on the Community page of the game.
